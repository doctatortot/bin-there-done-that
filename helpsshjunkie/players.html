🎧 Compatible Audio Players

Not all media players handle streaming the same way. If the web player isn’t working, you can use a standalone player that supports Icecast/MP3 streams.

🖥 Recommended Desktop Players

  • VLC Media Player (Windows, macOS, Linux)
  • Winamp (Windows)
  • Foobar2000 (Windows)
  • MPV (Linux/macOS, advanced users)

📱 Recommended Mobile Apps

  • VLC for Mobile (iOS & Android)
  • myTuner Radio (iOS & Android)
  • Radio Garden (Android, iOS, web)
  • XiiaLive (Android only, Icecast-compatible)

🔗 Direct Stream URLs

Paste this into your preferred player:

  • http://stream.genesis-radio.net:7454/stream

🧪 Tips

  • Make sure the app supports Icecast or SHOUTcast streams
  • Sometimes “Open Network Stream” or “Open URL” is the right option
  • If asked for a format, choose MP3
