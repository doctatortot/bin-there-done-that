Some networks (especially schools, workplaces, hotels, and public Wi-Fi) block streaming audio for bandwidth or content filtering reasons. If Genesis Radio works on your mobile data but not Wi-Fi, this may be the reason.

üì° Common Symptoms

  • Website loads but the stream never connects
  • Streaming apps timeout or fail silently
  • Everything works fine on mobile data or a different network

‚úÖ What You Can Try

  • Switch to mobile data (LTE/5G)
  • Try a VPN to bypass local network restrictions
  • If you're tech-savvy, change your DNS and test again (see DNS guide)
  • Ask your IT department (if applicable) to unblock ports:
    • 8000 (HTTP stream)
    • 8443 (HTTPS stream)

üéß Use Direct Stream Links

If the web player fails, try loading the stream directly in a media player:

  • http://stream.genesis-radio.net:8000/stream
  • https://stream.genesis-radio.net:8443/stream (SSL)

Open these in VLC, Winamp, or a modern browser.

üõ† Still Not Working?

Test from a different network, device, or VPN. If the stream plays there, it's almost certainly a network block on your primary connection.
