🌍 VPN & Geo-Blocking Issues

Sometimes VPNs or certain countries/ISPs may interfere with stream access due to how routing, firewalls, or blacklists are configured.

⚠️ Common Symptoms

  • Stream won't play while VPN is enabled
  • Website or stream works on mobile data but not Wi-Fi
  • Errors like “connection refused,” “cannot open file,” or endless loading

✅ What You Can Try

  • Disable your VPN temporarily and try again
  • Connect through a different VPN location (preferably in North America or Europe)
  • Try listening via direct stream links (see below)

🎧 Direct Stream Links

These links often work even when web-based players don’t:

  • http://stream.genesis-radio.net:8000/stream
  • https://stream.genesis-radio.net:8443/stream (SSL)

🧪 Still Blocked?

If the problem persists even without a VPN, check:

  • DNS settings
  • Firewall or local network restrictions
